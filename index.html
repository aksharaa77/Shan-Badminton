About Us

Founded in 2012, Shan Badminton School has grown into one of the most trusted and vibrant badminton academies in Sri Lanka. Under the guidance of our Head Coach, Shan, and a dedicated team of 6 experienced coaches, we are proud to have trained over 279 players across various skill levels.

With two professional courts and a structured training program, we cater to all age groups above 5 years â€“ from complete beginners to advanced players aiming to compete at higher levels. Our programs focus not only on developing strong technical skills but also on building discipline, fitness, and a true passion for the sport.

As an SLBA-affiliated academy (Registration No: PV 00288147), we actively encourage and prepare our players to participate in local and national tournaments, providing them with the right exposure and competitive spirit.

At Shan Badminton School, we believe every player has the potential to shine, and our mission is to create champions on and off the court.
